Before last October, I had never held a chicken in my life

It wasn't one of those bucket list things that people do, like traveling to every state in an RV or walking along the Great Wall of China.

I was on a friend's farm in southern Ohio and my friend asked me if I wanted to hold a chicken. Before I knew it I had a chicken in my arms.

If you've never held a chicken before, you're never really ready for it. Suddenly, the chicken is in your arms and you think, "okay, this really isn't bad."

I'd like to think this is a metaphor for life but maybe it's just about holding chickens.
